About Me!

I'm now a master student in cognitive neuroscience from East China Normal University (Shanghai, China) and involved in Memory, Attention and Cognition Lab advised by Professor Yixuan Ku. My research combines behavioral, EEG, fMRI and computational models to better understand the cognitive processes of visual working memory and attention. I'm particularly interested in memory decoding by some novel methods such as machine learning, deep learning and representational similarity analysis. In my spare time, I enjoy playing football and watch football game. My favorite team is Juventus.

NeuroRA

A Python Toolbox for Multimode Neural Data Representational Analysis

Representational Similarity Analysis (RSA) has become a popular and effective method to measure the representation of multivariable neural activity in different modes.

NeuroRA is an easy-to-use toolbox based on Python, which can do some works about RSA among nearly all kinds of neural data, including behavioral, EEG, MEG, fNIRS, ECoG, electrophysiological and fMRI data.
